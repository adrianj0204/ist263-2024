Cartoon Network first aired in October 1992, quickly becoming a beloved channel for animation fans of all ages.

Popular Shows:

  • The Powerpuff Girls: Blossom, Bubbles, and Buttercup fight crime and save Townsville.
  • Dexter's Laboratory: Follow Dexter as he navigates life as a boy genius.
  • Adventure Time: Journey with Finn and Jake in the Land of Ooo.

Fun Facts:

  • Cartoon Network was the first network to broadcast animated content 24/7.
  • The signature bouncing ball bumper is iconic to the brand.
